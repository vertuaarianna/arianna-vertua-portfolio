About

My creative journey began with abstract painting, where I developed a strong sensitivity to composition, color, balance, and visual expression. Over time, I translated this artistic foundation into graphic design, applying the same attention to aesthetics and storytelling to create posters, brochures, exhibition materials, and digital content.

Today, I combine creativity with strategic communication, designing social media campaigns and marketing materials that transform complex ideas into clear, engaging, and visually compelling experiences.
