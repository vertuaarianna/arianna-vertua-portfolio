Jewellery Craftsmanship

As part of my personal creative development, I completed a jewellery-making course at Scuola Orafa Ambrosiana in Milan, exploring the craftsmanship behind traditional silversmithing. Through the production of a sterling silver (925) piece—from metal melting and wire drawing to fabrication and final polishing—I developed a deeper appreciation for precision, material awareness and the value of meticulous execution.

Working with metal requires patience, accuracy and a strong attention to detail. These qualities have shaped my approach to creative work, encouraging thoughtful design decisions, a high standard of finish and a hands-on mindset that I bring to every visual communication project.
